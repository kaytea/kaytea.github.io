Hi, I'm Kate Wasynczuk

I'm a developer, designer, and maker who loves sharing the joys of technology with others. I'm a big fan of learning by doing and creating playful environments that encourage experimentation (you can learn a lot from breaking things!) When I'm not behind a screen, I'm looking for ways to create with whatever medium I can get my hands on - from watercolors to welding - or stepping outside to explore the great outdoors. I like almost any activity that involves water (scuba diving, sailing, windsurfing... you name it!) and I have a strong affinity for childrens toys in the educational technology space.
